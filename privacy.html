Privacy Policy

Bot: Zimowy Asystent

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Discord bot Zimowy Asystent collects, why it is collected, and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and process the following information:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Discord Role IDs
  • Discord Message IDs (when required)
  • Usernames and display names
  • Staff statistics
  • Voice activity statistics
  • Vacation records
  • Partner blacklist information
  • Moderation and security logs

The Bot does not collect passwords, email addresses, payment information, or personal information outside of Discord.

2. Why We Collect Data

Collected information is used exclusively to operate the Bot, including:

  • Server moderation
  • Anti-Nuke protection
  • Anti-Raid protection
  • Staff statistics
  • Automatic role management
  • Vacation management
  • Partner blacklist management
  • Slash commands
  • Security logging
  • Administrative utilities

3. Message Content

The Bot processes message content only when necessary to perform its intended functionality, including:

  • Executing commands
  • Moderation actions
  • Security systems
  • Anti-spam detection
  • Automatic responses
  • Staff reputation system

Message content is not permanently stored unless required by a moderation or security feature.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored locally using SQLite and is accessible only by the Bot owner.

5. Data Sharing

No collected data is sold, shared, rented, or transferred to third parties.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Bot's functionality.

Users may request removal of their stored data where applicable.

7. Security

Reasonable technical measures are implemented to protect stored data from unauthorized access.

8. User Rights

Users may request information about the data stored by the Bot or request its removal when technically possible.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the latest version.

10. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Bot owner via Discord.
